About MILCO

MILCO (Made In Lesotho Cooperative) is a supermarket and retail store based at Sefika Complex, Maseru. We sell only products manufactured in Lesotho.

Our goal is to help local farmers, artisans, and entrepreneurs reach customers through physical and online shopping platforms.
